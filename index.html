About Me

I'm currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Dr. Weitao Xu. My research interests include Wireless Sensing, LoRa Network, Energy Harvesting, and Cybersecurity. I received my M.E. degree at Australia National University (ANU), co-advised by Dr. Gabriela Ferraro and Dr. Qing Wang. I obtained my B.E. degree at the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering (SEIEE) at the Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Xinbing Wang. Meanwhile, I was admitted in 2014 ZHIYUAN Honors Program - Engineering (Top 10%).
